How SeaVoice Connects with SeaChat & SeaX

🔗 SeaChat Integration

SeaVoice Inbound Only ($29.99/month) is built on SeaChat Premium plan foundations, providing voice AI capabilities for incoming calls at $0.12 per minute.

Perfect for businesses wanting to add voice AI to their existing SeaChat setup.

📞 SeaX Integration

SeaVoice Inbound + Outbound ($99/month) mirrors SeaX Omni-channel pricing, enabling large-scale voice campaigns with callback handling.

Ideal for businesses running outbound campaigns who need comprehensive voice AI coverage.

✨ The Complete Picture

All Voice AI calls across SeaChat, SeaX, and SeaVoice use the same $0.12 per minute rate with ChatGPT-4o mini model. When contacts call back after outbound campaigns, SeaVoice seamlessly handles these inbound calls with the same AI intelligence.
