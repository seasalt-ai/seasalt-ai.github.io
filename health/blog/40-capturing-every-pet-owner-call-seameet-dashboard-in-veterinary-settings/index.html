Effective communication is a valuable asset in every business sector, including veterinary practices, creating and nurturing pet health. However, problems such as missed calls, and delayed responses result in frustrating the client and compromising pet care. The SeaMeet Dashboard included in SeaHealth is an AI tool that takes the lead as a powerful solution, assisting vets to capture every single pet owner call, boosting efficiency without testing their patience.

According to the Devonshire Digital Marketing Agency, 80% of callers won’t leave voicemails and will eventually contact another clinic if their call is not picked up on time. AI technology lets vets finally stop doing tasks such as manually updating medical records and get to spend more time with furry patients, according to AVMA. According to Veterinary Practice News, their vets have reported increased client understanding and satisfaction after using AI-driven tools.

Challenges in Veterinary Client Communications

Do Missed Calls and Delayed Responses Indicate Your Pet’s Health is Not Taken Seriously?

When phone lines are constantly busy, then naturally, you can’t expect the pet owner to wait for that long. Instead, they may switch to another medical facility where their pet’s health will be taken more seriously.

This leads to a big percentage of delayed responses to inquiries and appointment requests, spoiling the image of the medical facility.

Is Lack of Efficiency in Scheduling and Managing Information an Issue?

The manual scheduling of appointments always has room for errors, miscommunication while noting down the details of the patient, and even double bookings. This leaves a bad impression on the pet owners that the medical facility is not showing any kind of professionalism.

What problems does Zero Follow-Up Cause?

Pet parents feel quite agitated when there is zero follow-up for post-appointment care or time for refilling the prescribed medication, as that directly impacts their furry member’s health. So, yes, you can say that no proactive communication means a decrease in engaging the clients and losing their loyalty too.

How Are Satisfaction and Efficiency Impacted?

Once a few pet owners experience the same kind of non-serious attitude from the healthcare provider’s end, then you can easily expect that the anger will appear in the form of online negative reviews, discouraging other people from bringing in their pets as well. This results in imparting efficiency and satisfaction as staff time also gets wasted, while reducing overall practice and earnings as well.

Overview of the SeaMeet Dashboard

Is Call-Processing Handled Quickly?

The SeaMeet Dashboard puts every incoming and outgoing call into text, providing a written record to you. The platform summarizes the main key points and action items, providing a quick brief of the client interaction. Furthermore, it updates every 15 minutes, so you receive real-time insights for on-time responses.

How Does It Examine Client Interactions?

The SeaMeet Dashboard examines client interactions. How? It identifies the emotions of the client, elevating satisfaction levels and other possible concerns. The data from the call is analysed to track commonly asked inquiries while identifying areas where there is a need for improving service. Then, it has transcripts that can be searched for, letting you obtain information, elevate follow-ups, and keep records.

Key Features Beneficial to Veterinary Practices

The SeaMeet Dashboard offers the following key features beneficial to veterinary practices:

Call Reasons Analysis

The dashboard assists you in pinpointing frequently typed inquiries that pet parents are super eager to learn. In this way, resources are allocated and optimized, saving the hassle.

Agent Performance Evaluation

Agent performance evaluation allows you to track the scheduling success of the number of appointments with the conversion rates of the inquiries.

Lead Conversion Metrics

You might have heard about this term in digital marketing, but in the case of SeaMeet Dashboard, the leads are the phone interactions between the pet parents and the vet. And the conversions here are the number of appointments secured.

Customer Satisfaction Assessment

The dashboard’s main purpose is to provide you with a customer satisfaction assessment. Therefore, the AI tool gauges the sentiment of the client, guaranteeing that no call will be a negative one, and even your vet facility will receive positive words from all pet parents.

Medical Triage Monitoring

Unlike other AI tools, SeaMeet Dashboard offers you the feature of medical triage monitoring. What is that? It verifies proper phone triage, reducing liabilities while focusing on improving the care of the patient.

Warning Alerts

Another reason you will love using SeaMeet Dashboard is that it also offers warning alerts. For instance, it will flag any suspicious call activity, breach of data, and give you a message regarding controlling the quality.

Implementing the SeaMeet Dashboard in a Veterinary Setting

How to Integrate SeaMeet Dashboard In Your Veterinary Setting?

You need to connect the dashboard to your vet’s existing phone lines. After that, call your network provider to configure call routing and transfer of data.

How To Train Staff?

In this case, you must provide training to your staff so they can grasp the dashboard tutorials as soon as possible. This will help you strengthen a culture of tech adoption and receive prompt feedback from your staff as well.
