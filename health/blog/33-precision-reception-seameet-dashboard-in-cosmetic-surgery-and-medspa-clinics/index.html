Features of SeaMeet Dashboard

The SeaMeet Dashboard enhances communication within cosmetic surgery and MedSpa clinics by automating call tracking, optimizing receptionist performance, and boosting patient engagement through real-time analytics.

Comprehensive Call Analytics

The SeaMeet Dashboard transcribes and analyzes inbound and outbound calls, providing updates on patient interactions every 15 minutes. This real-time access to vital patient interaction data allows for better customer service through meeting customer service and trend identification with advanced systems using AI.

Identification of Call Reasons

Knowing the reason for a patient’s call is paramount to improving service. This feature identifies calls made for appointment booking, procedure inquiries, and post-procedure follow-ups. By identifying the main reason why patients call, clinics can adjust their response strategies.

Agent Performance Evaluation

Constant patient engagement with the clinic is maintained through tracking receptionist efficiency. The SeaMeet Dashboard evaluates the response rate of inquiries and converts appointments booked over the phone into scheduled appointments.

Lead Conversion Tracking

Every inquiry can convert to a customer for a MedSpa or a cosmetic surgery clinic. This feature measures lead conversion, providing insights into how calls are transformed into appointments. The conversion rate data informs training and enhances marketing efforts.

Customer Satisfaction Measurement

The system measures calls of agents for their tone, emotional sentiment from customers, and any mention of call-to-action. This measurement guarantees patients are serviced, which translates into engagement and enhanced retention.

Medical Triage Assessment

Proper phone triage decreases liability while enhancing the safety of the patient. The dashboard can monitor staff effort and adherence to proper procedures, thereby mitigating divergence in communication to the information relayed.

Warning Alerts

Taking an action regarding abnormal call patterns, noncompliance defined as not abiding by quality assurance parameters, quality and other pertinent considerations on the spot helps clinics manage problems before they arise, which could result in a loss of operational effectiveness.
