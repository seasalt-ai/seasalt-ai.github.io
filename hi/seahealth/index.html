Frequently Asked Questions

How does your front desk call analytics work?

We record your incoming and outgoing phone calls, then transcribe them for further analysis. Summary and alert emails are sent out on a daily basis.

Does it require recording the phone calls, and how?

We connect your VoIP provider to get the call recordings and do speech analysis on the recordings. If you use analog phones, we use a phone recording device that connects your call with phone jacks to get the recordings.

How does 24/7 phone call coverage work?

AI-powered receptionists handle calls round-the-clock, ensuring no missed opportunities. You can either pick up the call when they ring, or transfer the call to the AI receptionists during or after business hours.

Why should I use your service?

SeaHealth improves efficiency, enhances patient satisfaction, and increases revenue through automation and AI-powered solutions. It's cheaper, more reliable, and more empathetic than outsourcing your front desk receptionist work.
