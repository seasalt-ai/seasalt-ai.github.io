Frequently Asked Questions

What is WhatsApp Coexistence?
WhatsApp Coexistence is a feature that allows businesses to use both WhatsApp Business App and WhatsApp Cloud API on the same phone number, simultaneously. Messages are synced in real-time between both platforms, enabling you to keep the personal touch of the mobile app while leveraging the automation and scale of the API.
Do I need to change my phone number?
No! One of the key benefits of WhatsApp Coexistence is that you keep your existing phone number. You don't need to get a new number or change your existing one. The QR-code based onboarding process links your current WhatsApp Business App number to the Cloud API.
Will I lose my chat history?
No, you can preserve up to 180 days of chat history during onboarding. When you link your WhatsApp Business App to the Cloud API via the embedded signup process, you have the option to import your last six months of conversation history and contacts. However, this must be done within 24 hours of completing the onboarding process.
How does the pricing work?
WhatsApp Coexistence enables a hybrid pricing model. Messages sent manually from the WhatsApp Business App remain 100% free. Messages sent via the Cloud API (including automated messages, templates, and broadcasts) follow Meta's conversation-based pricing model. This allows you to optimize costs by using free app messaging for personal 1:1 conversations and API messaging for scale and automation.
What are the technical limitations?
The main technical limitations are: 1) Throughput is capped at 20 messages per second (MPS) for coexistence accounts, compared to higher limits for standalone API accounts; 2) You must open the WhatsApp Business App at least once every 13-14 days to maintain the sync connection; 3) Some app features are disabled, including group chats syncing, disappearing messages, view-once media, and message editing/revoking.
Is it available in all countries?
No, WhatsApp Coexistence is currently in a phased rollout. As of January 2026, it's not available in certain regions including Nigeria and South Africa. Businesses with phone numbers from these countries are currently ineligible and will need to choose between standalone App or standalone API.
How long does migration take?
The migration process is designed to be quick and seamless. The QR-code based embedded signup flow typically takes 5-10 minutes to complete. If you choose to import chat history, this will happen in the background. The entire process can be completed in under 15 minutes with no disruption to your ongoing WhatsApp operations.
What happens if I stop using it?
You can discontinue using the Cloud API at any time. Your WhatsApp Business App will continue to work normally with your number. However, you'll lose access to the automation, multi-agent features, and CRM integration that the API provided. You won't be able to reactivate coexistence for that number until you contact Meta support and complete a new onboarding process.
